M.B.A
👇

SNMV Institute of management was started by the Coimbatore Welfare Association in 1994 during the silver jubilee year of CWA. SNMV Institute of Management – the Management Studies wing of SNMV family of Educational Institutions, is today ranked as one of the best Management Institutes under Bharathiar University. SNMV Institute of Management, a not-for-profit education institution offers a full-time MBA Programme. The institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to Bharathiar University, Coimbatore.
