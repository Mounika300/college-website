COURSES OFFERED





WLCOME SNMV

The untiring efforts of CWA have enabled SNMV to blossom into a centre of learning. Today, it offers a rich array of UG and PG programmes along with Research and Doctoral programmes complemented by fourteen Value Added Courses sponsored by the UGC. The present strength of the college is 3279 students and 147 teaching faculty members and 100 non-teaching staff members. The college is approved by AICTE and is affiliated to Bharathiar University, Coimbatore. Various undergraduate programs are available, such as Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Science (BSc), Bachelor of Commerce (BCom), and others, catering to diverse academic interests and career paths.







B.COM (CA)
👇

The department of Commerce with Computer Applications B.Com Computer Applications combines commerce with computer science and information technology. Students learn to use technology to streamline business processes, manage data, and develop software solutions for accounting and financial operations. Graduates can work as software developers, systems analysts, database administrators. This will make them easy to progress their career by doing computerized work in the fields of Finance and Accounts.

B.COM
👇

The B.Com General offers a broad and versatile curriculum covering fundamental concepts in commerce, including accounting, finance, marketing, management, and economics. It provides a well-rounded understanding of business principles. The department offers a diverse and comprehensive educational program designed to prepare students for a wide range of careers in the field of commerce. The B.Com program provides a solid foundation in business and equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in various professional settings.

B.COM(IT)
👇

The Department of B.Com (IT) was established in the year 2009. The department focuses on the application of information technology in the field of commerce. Students learn about IT infrastructure, software applications for business, and the effective use of technology in various business operations. Graduates can pursue careers as IT managers, business analysts, network administrators, or work as IT consultants for businesses.
